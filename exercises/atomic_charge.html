An atom consists of NEUTRONS neutrons, PROTONS protons and ELECTRONS electrons.

What is the charge of this atom?

CHARGE

The charge of an atom is determined by the number of electrons and protons it has.

The charge of each proton is +1 and the charge of each electron is -1.

The atom therefore consists of PROTONS positive charges and ELECTRONS negative charges.

Thus, the overall charge of this atom is PROTONS * 1 + ELECTRONS*-1 = CHARGE.
